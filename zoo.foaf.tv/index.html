a curated aggregation of terms from popular vocabularies

This profile integrates a collection of terms (properties and types) from independently managed descriptive vocabularies.

It is not intended to be complete, comprehensive or universally useful. But in many circumstances it may save publishers the trouble of remembering vocabulary prefixes or URLs. It complements the work on data-driven common prefix definitions by providing a richer level of syntactic integration amongst a package of vocabularies. This is an experiment, and is not exclusive. Other profiles can be used instead, or in addition. Terms can always be used via their full URL, or by prefix.
